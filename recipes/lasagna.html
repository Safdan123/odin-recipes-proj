Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella.

Baked until bubbly and golden, it combines savory flavors and textures into a comforting, hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Fresh parsley
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh parsley. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  11. Repeat layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  12. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  13. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
